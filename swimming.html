We are both a social and competitive club, welcoming all personalities and abilities!

The fun, exciting and inclusive atmosphere within the swimming and water polo club is what makes us so successful. Both our male and female water polo teams compete within the BUCS Leagues and in the BUCS Cup. We also attend multiple social events throughout the year for those who wish to be involved but less competitive. Our annual Granite City Cup, which is an invitational water polo tournament with other universities across Scotland hosted by us, is one of the highlights of the year.
