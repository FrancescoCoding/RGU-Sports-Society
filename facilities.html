Facilities

Come visit us!

RGU Sport Facilities:

Gym - We have a variety of different gym suites, all of which have state of the art gym equipment, interactive media centres and an online virtual training system.

Swimming - RGU SPORT is home to a 25 metre swimming pool with 6 lanes, hosting a variety of different events throughout the year.

Climbing - We have a state of the art 11m climbing wall and a bouldering room to offer climbing courses, taster or refresher sessions, kids clubs and assessments.

Visit us today to sign up for sport courses and classes.
Located in Garthdee, on the banks of the River Dee,
our modern facilities offer stunning views
of the river and surrounding woodlands. Access is very easy,
with just a 10 minute drive from the city center,
or a number 1 or 2 bus from Union Street.
